Review

Topics Covered: Sessions and cookies; the session hash; user authentication; using the Devise gem

Exercises

E1: Cookies and Sessions

Write down your answers to the following:

  1. What are cookies? Where are they stored?
  2. Why do we use cookies?
  3. Why are cookies encrypted? What could a malicious person do if they got a hold of your cookies?
  4. Are cookies sent with every browser request? Are all of them sent, or only a subset?

E2: Devise a Solution

Recreate the codealong from scratch. You can use the lecture slides, the Code on Github, the RailsCast, or the Devise documentation for reference. Add links to be able to sign up, sign in and sign out.

Customization

Customize Devise to get rid of the "forgot password?" and "remember me?" links.

E3: Revise your Twitter

Use Devise to add user accounts and authentication to working_twitter (code here). Make it so anyone can view all the tweets, but you can only add and delete tweets if you're logged in. Note: you'll have to destroy and regenerate the User model, or modify it significantly and write new migrations!
